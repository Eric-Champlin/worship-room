### Step 3 (CONDITIONAL on R1=(b)): Apply route-aware className to AudioPill

```tsx
className={`fixed z-[${Z.AUDIO_PILL}] flex h-14 min-w-[44px] items-center gap-3 rounded-full border border-white/[0.12] px-4 transition-opacity motion-reduce:transition-none duration-base bottom-0 left-1/2 -translate-x-1/2 mb-[max(24px,calc(env(safe-area-inset-bottom)+8px))] ${prayerWallBottomOffset} lg:left-auto lg:right-6 lg:bottom-6 lg:translate-x-0 lg:mb-0`}
```

**Now-playing variant (around line 112):**

Same replacement — add `${prayerWallBottomOffset}` after `mb-[...]` and before the `lg:` overrides.

**Cascade reasoning (critical — do not invert):**
- Tailwind's mobile-first cascade means `bottom-0` (mobile default) is overridden by `bottom-24` (when added).
- `lg:bottom-6` is a `lg:` prefix that wins at `≥1024px`. At desktop xl+ (`≥1280px`) the sticky filter row does NOT render (`<div className="xl:hidden">` wrapper at `PrayerWall.tsx:1192`).
- So the conditional `bottom-24` injection takes effect at mobile/tablet, and `lg:bottom-6` continues to win at lg+ where it's not needed for overlap protection but doesn't hurt.
- Order matters in the className string. `bottom-24` should appear AFTER `bottom-0` and BEFORE `lg:bottom-6` so Tailwind's specificity cascade produces the right output.
