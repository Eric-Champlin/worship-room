### Discovery 2 — Brief's proposed `<div>` + PageHero + CinematicHeroBackground composition has two structural conflicts (W2 / W8 / W15)

Reading `frontend/src/components/PageHero.tsx` (verified line-by-line) shows PageHero internals that the brief did not fully account for:

1. **PageHero's outer `<section>` carries `style={ATMOSPHERIC_HERO_BG}`** which paints:
   - `backgroundColor: '#0f0a1e'` (opaque dashboard-dark) +
   - `backgroundImage: radial-gradient(... rgba(109,40,217,0.15) ...)` (the 15% violet radial)

   The opaque base color **masks CinematicHeroBackground** sitting behind it in the DOM. Brief D2 option (c) says "accept additive composition," but additive composition is geometrically impossible when one layer is fully opaque — additive means transparent-over-something. The cinematic atmospheric (stars, warm beam, nebula tint, film grain) would not be visible behind PageHero's painted section. **W2 violation.**

2. **PageHero's `<section>` carries `pt-32 pb-8 sm:pt-36 sm:pb-12 lg:pt-40`** (128/144/160 px top + 32/48 px bottom). Wrapping PageHero in `<div className="relative w-full pt-[145px]">` stacks an additional 145px on top of PageHero's own 128-160px → total top padding **273-305px** on mobile/tablet/desktop, compared to the current hero's 145px. The composer block below would shift down by ~130-160px. **W8 violation.**

Brief Section 7 D2 says: "If plan-time visual review finds the result too dark or busy, escalate; default decision is to accept it." Recon escalates: the issue is not "too dark" or "too busy," it's that PageHero's opaque base color geometrically blocks the cinematic AND the layout shifts ~130-160px below the hero.

**Brief W15 forbids modifying PageHero internals** (no `transparent: true` prop addition), which closes the cleanest fix.

## Architecture Context

**Hero family on Worship Room (post-Spec-14 — see `09-design-system.md` § "Cinematic Hero Pattern"):**

Two distinct hero patterns coexist by design. They are **not interchangeable**:

| Pattern | Pages | Atmospheric layer | Padding | Heading sizing |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **PageHero** (component) | `/music/routines` (verbatim), `/settings`, `/insights` (consume `ATMOSPHERIC_HERO_BG` constant inline) | Opaque `#0f0a1e` + 15% violet radial via `style={ATMOSPHERIC_HERO_BG}` | `pt-32 pb-8 sm:pt-36 sm:pb-12 lg:pt-40` (responsive) | `text-4xl sm:text-5xl lg:text-6xl` via internal h1 with GRADIENT_TEXT_STYLE |
| **Cinematic** (per-page rolls-own) | `/daily`, `/bible` (BibleHero), `/local-support/*` (LocalSupportHero), `/ask`, `/grow`, `/prayer-wall` (PrayerWallHero), `/music` (MusicHero) | `CinematicHeroBackground` (memo'd 8-layer composition, mask-fade-to-transparent at base) | `pt-[145px] pb-12` constant (no responsive — navbar height is fixed) | Same h1 sizing, rolls-own per page |

The brief's "compose PageHero internally" instruction mixes these two families. PageHero's opaque base color and responsive padding are incompatible with the cinematic family's transparent atmospheric and constant 145px clearance. See Discovery 2 above.

**SectionHeading consumers (homepage + RegisterPage):**

- `JourneySection`: `topLine="Your Journey to" bottomLine="Healing"` (3 words / 1 word)
- `DashboardPreview`: `topLine="See How You're" bottomLine="Growing"` (3 words / 1 word)
- `DifferentiatorSection`: `topLine="Built for" bottomLine="Your Heart"` (2 words / 2 words)
- `RegisterPage`: legacy single-line `heading="..."` mode (not 2-line)

Calibration: topLines are short (2-3 words), declarative-neutral, present-tense or possessive. No emoji, no exclamation, no comparison framing.
